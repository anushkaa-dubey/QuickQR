How to Use QuickQR

Create stunning QR codes in just a few simple steps

1

Input Your Content

Start by selecting the type of content you want to encode. Choose between URL, Text, Contact Card, or use our built-in scanner.

2

Customize Appearance

Use our advanced customization tools to make your QR code unique:

• Adjust colors and size
• Add your logo
• Choose error correction level

3

Download & Share

Once satisfied with your design, download in multiple formats or share directly to social media platforms.
